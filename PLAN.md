# Diet Studio — VSL Landing Page Plan

> **Client:** Diet Studio
> **Goal:** Convert visitors into paid consultations at ₹200/session
> **Format:** Video Sales Letter (VSL) Landing Page
> **Target Audience:** Indians struggling with weight loss, weight gain, maintenance, diabetes, PCOD, thyroid & other diet-related health issues

---

## 1. Project Overview

### What We're Building
A single-page VSL (Video Sales Letter) website that:
- Hooks the visitor with a compelling headline + short explainer video
- Educates them on why generic diets fail
- Presents Diet Studio as the personalized solution
- Collects ₹200 consultation fee via Razorpay
- Books a 1-on-1 consultation slot automatically

### Business Logic
| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Consultation Fee | ₹200 (one-time, just to book the slot) |
| Diet Plan Pricing | Discussed/sold separately during the consultation |
| Consultation Format | Video call (Zoom/Google Meet) or Phone |
| Booking Flow | CTA → Form → Payment → Slot booking → Confirmation |

### Section 9 — FAQ (Objection Handling)
**Goal:** Answer every hesitation before it blocks the sale.

```
Q1: "Is ₹200 just for the consultation? What does the diet plan cost?"
A: Yes, ₹200 is only the consultation booking fee. During your session,
   your dietitian will explain the personalized plan options and their
   pricing. There's no obligation to purchase anything else.

Q2: "I'm vegetarian. Will the diet plan work for me?"
A: Absolutely! All our plans are designed around your food preferences —
   vegetarian, non-vegetarian, Jain, regional cuisine, anything.

Q3: "I have a medical condition (diabetes, thyroid, PCOD). Can you help?"
A: Yes! This is our specialty. Our dietitians are trained to create plans
   for medical conditions. Please mention your condition while booking.

Q4: "How is this different from random diet charts on YouTube?"
A: Generic charts don't know your body, your blood reports, your
   lifestyle, or your food habits. We do a complete assessment first.

Q5: "What if I'm not satisfied with the consultation?"
A: We're confident you'll get real value. But if you're not satisfied,
   reach out to us and we'll make it right.

Q6: "How is the consultation conducted?"
A: Via phone call or video call (Zoom/Google Meet). You'll choose your
   preferred slot after payment.

Q7: "How quickly will I see results?"
A: Most clients notice improvements in energy and health markers within
   2-4 weeks. Weight results vary, but our plans are designed for
   sustainable, long-term success.
```

## 4. Technical Stack

### Recommended Tech

| Layer | Choice | Reason |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Framework | **Next.js 14 (App Router)** | Fast, SEO-friendly, easy deployment |
| Styling | **Tailwind CSS** | Rapid development, responsive by default |
| Animations | **Framer Motion** | Smooth scroll animations, hero animations |
| Payment | **Razorpay** | #1 payment gateway in India, simple integration |
| Booking | **Cal.com** (self-hosted or hosted) OR custom slot picker | Free, easy to embed |
| Forms | **React Hook Form + Zod** | Validation + type safety |
| Email | **Resend / Nodemailer** | Send confirmation emails post-payment |
| Deployment | **Vercel** | Zero-config Next.js hosting |
| Analytics | **Google Analytics 4 + Meta Pixel** | Track conversions |

## 6. Design Direction

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary Green | `#2D6A4F` | Headers, primary buttons |
| Light Green | `#74C69D` | Accents, icons |
| Warm Cream | `#F8F4E3` | Background sections |
| Dark Text | `#1A1A2E` | Body text |
| Gold/Yellow | `#F4A261` | Urgency elements, highlights |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Cards, contrast sections |

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Playfair Display or Merriweather (authoritative, health feel)
- **Body:** Inter or Poppins (clean, readable)
- **Hindi Text (if used):** Hind Siliguri (Google Font, free)

### Visual Style
- Clean, minimal, trustworthy (not flashy)
- Real food photography (Indian dishes)
- Professional dietitian photo
- Before/after result cards
- Green leaf motifs as design accents
- Mobile-first layout

## 12. Questions Before Starting

1. Does the client want the page in **Hindi**, **English**, or **Hinglish** (mix)?
2. Does the client have a **logo** already?
3. Is the **VSL video** ready, or do we need to script/record it?
4. Does the client want a **WhatsApp booking option** in addition to online payment?
5. Which **conditions** should we highlight most (weight loss? diabetes? PCOD)?
6. Does the client have an existing **Razorpay account**?
7. What is the client's **consultation format** — phone call or video call?
8. Should the ₹200 be shown as a **limited-time offer** or permanent pricing?
